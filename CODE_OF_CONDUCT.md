# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people who participate in this project. We expect all members of our community to conduct themselves professionally and respectfully toward one another.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Respectful Communication:** Treating all community members with respect and dignity
- **Openness:** Being open to different viewpoints, experiences, and backgrounds
- **Constructive Feedback:** Providing and accepting feedback constructively
- **Inclusivity:** Making everyone feel welcome regardless of experience level
- **Patience:** Being patient with others who are learning
- **Focus:** Keeping discussions relevant and productive

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behaviors include:

- **Harassment:** Offensive comments, intimidation, or personal attacks
- **Discrimination:** Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic
- **Abuse:** Verbal, physical, or sexual abuse
- **Trolling:** Deliberately disruptive or provocative behavior
- **Spam:** Excessive self-promotion or off-topic content
- **Privacy Violations:** Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Community participants who violate this code of conduct will be:

1. **First Offense:** Warned privately by project maintainers
2. **Second Offense:** Temporary suspension from community participation
3. **Repeated Violations:** Permanent removal from the community

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers immediately.

**Report to:** [Open an issue](https://github.com/Aashish-po/GitHub__Badge/issues) or contact maintainers directly

All reports will be:
- Investigated promptly
- Handled confidentially
- Treated respectfully

## Attribution

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (issues, discussions, pull requests)
- Community events
- Project-related communication

Thank you for helping us maintain a welcoming, inclusive community! 🤝
