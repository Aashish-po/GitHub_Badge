# 🌠 Galaxy Brain Badge - SVG Assets

## About the Badge

The **Galaxy Brain** badge recognizes helpful answers in GitHub Discussions.

### Badge Overview

- **Name:** Galaxy Brain
- **Variants:** Bronze (1 answer), Silver (10 answers), Gold (25 answers)
- **Achievement Type:** *Medium* ⭐⭐⭐
- **Focus:** Community support and knowledge sharing

## Badge Progression

| Tier | Answers | Achievement |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 🥉 Bronze | 1 accepted answer | Galaxy Brain Badge (Bronze) |
| 🥈 Silver | 10 accepted answers | Galaxy Brain Badge (Silver) |
| 🥇 Gold | 25 accepted answers | Galaxy Brain Badge (Gold) |

## How to Earn

### Genius Method (Recommended)

1. **Find active repositories:**
   - Popular frameworks (React, Next.js, Node.js)
   - Community-focused projects
   - Well-maintained codebases

2. **Answer thoughtfully:**
   - Read the question carefully
   - Provide detailed, tested solutions
   - Include code examples
   - Format your answer well

3. **Get marked as accepted:**
   - Maintainers or questioners mark helpful answers
   - Answer within 24 hours (higher chance)
   - Be kind and patient

**Timeline:**
- Bronze: 1-2 weeks
- Silver: 2-4 months
- Gold: 6+ months

## Tips for Success

✅ **Do:**
- Test your solution before answering
- Provide step-by-step explanations
- Include working code examples
- Answer within 24 hours
- Be polite and encouraging

❌ **Don't:**
- Copy answers from StackOverflow
- Provide untested solutions
- Be dismissive of questions
- Spam discussions
